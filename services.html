Green Energy (Solar Solutions)

At Sun Harvesters, we specialize in delivering clean, reliable solar energy solutions designed to meet the unique power needs of homes, businesses, and institutions. Our professionally engineered systems help clients significantly reduce electricity costs, minimize environmental impact, and achieve greater energy independence. Whether you require an off-grid, hybrid, or grid-tied solution, our installations are tailored for efficiency, performance, and long-term sustainability. As a Strategic Business Unit under Stripe Facility Management Limited, we bring together industry expertise and innovative technology to provide end-to-end solar energy services—from consultation and design to installation and ongoing support. Our commitment is to deliver dependable, future-ready energy systems that empower our clients with consistent power and peace of mind.

Smart Home Integration

Experience the future of modern living through intelligent home automation. At Sun Harvesters, we transform conventional spaces into fully connected smart homes that combine comfort, convenience, and control. Our solutions seamlessly integrate lighting, security systems, climate control, entertainment units, and household appliances bringing everything under your command. Using your smartphone, tablet, or voice assistant, you can manage and monitor your home in real-time, whether you're across the room or across the world. Dim the lights, adjust the temperature, view security footage, or start your coffee maker with a simple tap or voice command.

Security Surveillance Systems

Protect what matters most with Sun Harvesters' advanced surveillance solutions, designed to give you complete confidence and control over your environment. We offer a comprehensive range of high performance security systems, including HD CCTV cameras, remote access monitoring, smart motion detection, and real time alerts all engineered to keep your property secure, day and night. Whether you're safeguarding a home, office, retail space, or industrial facility, our systems are scalable and fully customizable to suit your specific security needs. With cloud based storage options and mobile integration, you can monitor live feeds, playback recordings, and receive instant notifications from anywhere in the world using your smartphone or tablet.
